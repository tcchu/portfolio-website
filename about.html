About

When I'm designing, I seek out the correct problems to solve backed by user research and strategically map out task flows to ensure the design has utility and is user friendly.

I am a turned UX Designer, previously a Test Engineer and Engineering Project Manager. I enjoy iterating designs followed by usability testing to ensure user problems are being met with the correct solution.

With a mixed background in Mechanical Engineering and Business, I have a holistic approach to bringing products from concept to launch.
